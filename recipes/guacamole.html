Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a vibrant green dip with Mexican roots. Its creamy texture comes from mashed avocado, the star ingredient. Simple additions like chopped onion, tomato, lime juice, cilantro, and salt create a refreshing and flavorful combination. Traditionally enjoyed with tortilla chips for scooping, guacamole's versatility extends to topping tacos, burritos, salads, or even toast, adding a burst of creamy goodness to any dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
