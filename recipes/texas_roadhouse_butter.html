Copycat Texas Roadhouse Butter

Description

Craving the honey butter magic from Texas Roadhouse? This copycat recipe captures the essence of that sweet, slightly cinnamon-y goodness. It starts with softened butter for easy spreading, then whips in honey for a touch of sweetness. A sprinkle of cinnamon adds warmth and depth, creating a delightful spread perfect for warm bread, rolls, or even pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
