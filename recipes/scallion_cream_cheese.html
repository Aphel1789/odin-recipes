Scallion Cream Cheese

Description

Scallion cream cheese is a simple yet delightful spread. Chopped scallions (green onions) add a mild oniony bite and vibrant green flecks to rich, creamy cheese. Perfect for bagels, crackers, or even veggies, it's a versatile and flavorful topping

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) container whipped cream cheese, softened
  • 2 scallions, white and green parts, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon white pepper

Steps

  1. Place softened cream cheese in a bowl. Mix in scallions, salt, garlic powder, and white pepper. Mixture can be returned to the original cream cheese container for storage.
